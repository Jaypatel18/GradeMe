FAQ!

What is GradeMe?
Its a student academic tool.
What does GradeMe do?
Calculate your GPA, you can chat with your classmates, you can see what you need to get a desired grade in the class, you can upload study guide and rate them.
How to use GradeMe?
Go to NavBar and click on the section that you would like to play with or use.
Is it Free to use?
Yes, its absolutley free to use, there are some premium chargers if you would like to take an advantage of latest exams etc.
How do GradeMe make money?
We have a premium account where students will have to pay for the account.
Can I chat with anyone in class?
Yes, you can chat with anyone in class or whoever is online and using GradeMe application throughout the campus.
Why can't you text someone in class
You are either blocked or not allowed to chat with the specific person in this software.
